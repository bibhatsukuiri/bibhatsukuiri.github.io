Profile

Academic Overview

I work in physics with an emphasis on numerical modelling and the design of optical and photonic systems. My interests include translating physical principles into reproducible computational models, evaluating device behaviour, and developing scientifically grounded sensing concepts. This website serves as a concise record of my research interests and academic profile.

Research

Areas of Interest

My research interests are centred on computational physics, photonic device analysis, and optical sensing, with particular attention to clear physical interpretation and reliable numerical methods.

01

Photonic Structures

Numerical study of wave propagation, resonant structures, photonic crystals, and multilayer optical systems.

02

Optical Sensing

Design and analysis of refractive-index, biochemical, thermal, and material-sensitive optical sensors.

03

Computational Physics

Development of numerical models for electromagnetic, thermal, and coupled physical phenomena.

04

Multiphysics Modelling

Simulation-led investigation of interacting physical processes in scientific and engineering systems.
