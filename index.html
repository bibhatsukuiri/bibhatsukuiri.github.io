Research Focus

Current Academic Areas

My work combines physics-based modelling, computational design, experimentation, and machine-learning methods for optical, material, and coupled engineering systems.

Computational Photonics

Photonic crystal fibres, resonant structures, multilayer systems, mode analysis, dispersion, and confinement engineering.

THz and OAM Systems

Low-loss multimode THz guidance, orbital-angular-momentum transmission, bending tolerance, and communication architectures.

Optical Sensors

Photonic-crystal, SPR, refractive-index, biochemical, thermal, and material-sensitive sensing platforms.

Multiphysics Modelling

Electromagnetic, structural, thermal, cardiac, and coupled finite-element models for scientific applications.

AI for Physical Systems

Surrogate models, deep-learning prediction, optimisation, scientific data analysis, and simulation-assisted design.

Energy and Materials

Solid-state batteries, lithium dendrite suppression, energy-storage materials, glasses, nanomaterials, and composites.
