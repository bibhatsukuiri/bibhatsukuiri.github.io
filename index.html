Featured Publication

Direct correlation between void formation and lithium dendrite growth in solid-state electrolytes with interlayers

Nature Materials, 2022

This work established that interfacial void formation precedes lithium dendrite nucleation in garnet-based solid-state batteries. By combining electrochemical experiments, microscopy, and first-principles calculations, it showed how suitable metallic interlayers can suppress void growth and improve dendrite tolerance without impractically high stack pressure.

Terahertz Photonics

Ultra-low loss polymer-based photonic crystal fiber supporting 242 OAM modes with high bending tolerance for multimode THz communication

Results in Physics, 2022

A polymer ring-core photonic crystal fibre was designed to support 242 stable orbital-angular-momentum modes near 1.9 THz. The architecture combined very low confinement loss, high mode purity, broad THz operation, and strong bending tolerance for high-capacity multimode communication.

AI-Assisted Photonics

Deep learning prediction of novel hollow core photonic crystal fiber with tuned As2S3-LiNbO3 ring for multimode applications

Optical Fiber Technology, 2024

This study introduced a dual-ring hollow-core fibre supporting more than 200 OAM modes across the near-infrared region. A deep-learning model reproduced the numerically simulated fibre response within about 5 percent, demonstrating a practical route toward faster photonic design and performance prediction.
